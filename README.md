Ever wondered how to stop lazing off and get the motivation you need to actually get to work ? Well look no further, as I shall present you with the new Productivity Enhancer 3000™, a simple yet efficient way to boost your productivity and work efficiency by sending you to McDonald's Job application webpage every time you start lazing off for too long :)

How this works:
The productivity bar slowly goes down when you're not typing and it refills when you do (which keeps you working ig)
The unemployment rate® slider allows you to choose your prefered difficulty, as the higher it is, the faster the productivity wil decrease.
Once you reach 30% productivity, a warning pop-up will appear to trashtalk you into getting back to work.
If you don't, and you're productivity hits zero, a random webpage from the following list will open and make your path towards your not so bright future at McDonald's easier for you !

(This project was born out of a peculiar form of self-awareness, the kind that sits quietly at the back of your mind, whispering uncomfortable truths you'd rather not confront head-on. Somewhere between writing the code and debugging the third typo in ctypes.windll, I came to terms with the fact that this program was never really about productivity. It was about the uneasy suspicion that my trajectory, if left unchecked, leads somewhere with a drive-through window and a paper hat.
There is a certain irony in spending hours building a tool designed to punish idleness, while being idle enough to think of it in the first place. But perhaps that's the point. The Productivity Enhancer 3000™ is less a utility and more a mirror, one that counts down in red, warns you when you've been staring too long at nothing, and, in its final act of tough love, submits your McDonald's application for you.
Consider it a preemptive intervention. A gift to my future self, who will probably need the job.)
